About Sunny Park Langebaan

Discover the West Coast's premier family entertainment destination

Our Story

Since 1988 Sunnypark offers a wide range of activities for young and old in a safe environment. Situated in the center of Langebaan, opposite the Total Garage and Spar, with ample free parking. We are open every weekend and all school holidays throughout the year. Sunnypark is the perfect venue for birthday parties and group outings. We have a special package for groups of more than 10 people. All group bookings must be booked in advance. For a day filled with fun and entertainment, Sunny Park is the place to be. Our fun park activities include 5 heated swimming pools; (Super Tube, Slip n Slide, Kiddies Swimming Pool, Big Swimming Pool and the Indoor Heated Pool). Other activities include paintball target shooting, trampolines, 9 hole mini golf, pool tables, pedal karts and a kidz zone soft play area. Braai facilities are available for that perfect family and friends braai day.

Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional family entertainment that brings joy to our community and visitors from across South Africa.
